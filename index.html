The Metropolitan Museum of Art Visualizations

Located in the heart of New York City, the Metropolitan Museum of Art is the largest museum in the United States. The permanent collection consists of artwork created from Ancient Egypt, Ancient Asia, Medieval Europe, to contemporary American art, attracting tourists from all over the world.

The Met Visualizations is a data visualization project that curates the art works digitally, demonstrates the Met collection in digestible ways and provides interactive art viewing experience for the audience.

Divided into four sections, the website will tour the audience first by the origins of the artworks(1), followed with time period(2), then by a word cloud of artwork made from different materials(3), and finally present an artwork showcase of a few selected Met artists(4).

Data source from the Met Open Access.
